# Highwoods Community App

A hyperlocal community platform connecting residents in Highwoods and surrounding areas. Buy, sell, discover local events, stay safe, and build meaningful connections with your neighbours.

## Features

### Authentication & User Management
- **Magic Link Sign-in** - Passwordless email authentication for quick, secure access
- **Invitation-Only Registration** - Grow the community through trusted referrals
- **User Profiles** - Customizable profiles with avatar, bio, and username
- **Verified Badges** - Visual indicators for admin and moderator accounts

### Community Feed

The heart of the app - a dynamic feed with **20+ post types** across 6 categories:

#### Marketplace
- **For Sale** - List items you want to sell locally
- **Free / Freecycle** - Give away items to neighbours
- **Looking to Buy** - Post what you're searching for
- **Borrow / Rent** - Share resources within the community

Features include price display, item condition, pickup/delivery options, and negotiable pricing.

#### Recommendations
- **Trades** - Recommend local services (plumbers, electricians, builders)
- **Lifestyle** - Share favourite restaurants, activities, and local tips
- **Looking for Help** - Request assistance from the community
- **Offering Help** - Advertise services you can provide

Includes business ratings, price ranges, and contact information.

#### Safety & Alerts
- **Urgent Alerts** - Immediate safety concerns requiring attention
- **Security Alerts** - Report suspicious activity and security issues

Priority-based system with sticky alerts that stay visible at the top of the feed.

#### Lost & Found
- **Lost Pet / Found Pet** - Help reunite pets with their owners
- **Lost Item / Found Item** - Track down misplaced belongings

Supports pet details (name, breed, colour), last seen location, contact info, and reward offerings.

#### Events & Social
- **Events** - Create and discover local gatherings with RSVP support
- **Find a Partner** - Connect for hobbies and activities
- **Discussion** - General community Q&A and conversations

Full event management with date/time, venue, maximum attendees, and RSVP tracking.

#### Jobs & Services
- **Looking for Work** - Share your availability and skills
- **Hiring** - Post local job opportunities

Includes job type (one-time, recurring, part-time, full-time), pay rates, required skills, and experience levels.

### Community Interaction
- **Threaded Comments** - Nested discussions with reply support
- **Reactions** - Express yourself with Like, Love, Helpful, and Thanks
- **Save Posts** - Bookmark content for later
- **Search** - Find posts across the community

### Direct Messaging
- **One-to-One Chat** - Private conversations between neighbours
- **Message Author** - Contact post creators directly
- **Read Receipts** - Know when messages have been seen
- **Conversation History** - Full message persistence

### Connections & Networking
- **Connection Requests** - Send and receive friend requests
- **Connection Status** - Track pending, accepted, and blocked connections
- **Network Building** - Grow your local community network

### Invite System
- **Generate Invite Links** - Create shareable invitation codes
- **Native Sharing** - Share via WhatsApp, SMS, or Email
- **Track Invitations** - Monitor pending and accepted invites
- **Custom Messages** - Personalize invitation notes

### Business Directory
- **Local Listings** - Discover neighbourhood businesses and services
- **Categories** - Services, Events, Organisations, Products
- **Ratings & Reviews** - Community testimonials
- **Rich Profiles** - Images, videos, contact details, and websites

### Notifications
- **Push Notifications** - Stay updated via OneSignal integration
- **Granular Controls** - Customize notifications for:
  - New posts
  - Comments on your content
  - Direct messages
  - Connection requests
  - Event updates
  - Safety alerts

### Theme Customization
- **7 Theme Options** - Light, Dark, Forest Green, Sea Blue, Pink, Black & Orange, High Contrast
- **Accessibility** - High contrast mode for improved visibility
- **Persistent Preferences** - Theme choice saved across sessions

### Deep Linking
- **Magic Link Authentication** - Seamless email sign-in
- **Share Posts** - Direct links to specific content
- **Invitation Links** - One-tap community joining
- **Universal Links** - Works across platforms

## Technical Highlights

- **Flutter** - Cross-platform iOS and Android support
- **Material Design 3** - Modern, accessible UI
- **Supabase Backend** - Real-time data synchronization
- **Riverpod State Management** - Reactive, scalable architecture
- **OneSignal** - Reliable push notification delivery
