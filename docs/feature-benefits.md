# Highwoods Community App — Features & Benefits

A private, invitation-only community app for residents and people who work in Highwoods (Colchester, UK). It helps neighbours buy/sell, stay safe, find help, and build genuine local connections.

## Positioning
A hyperlocal, invitation-only social app for Highwoods that keeps community life practical, safe, and neighbourly.

## Who It’s For
Residents and people working in Highwoods who want a trusted, local-first way to connect with their community.

## Core Features & Benefits

### 1) Invitation-Only Access
Features
- Invitation-only registration and shareable invite links
- Magic-link sign-in (passwordless email login)
- Track invites and send custom invite messages

Benefits
- Builds trust by keeping the community local and verified
- Low-friction onboarding without passwords
- Growth stays intentional and neighbour-driven

### 2) Community Feed with Rich Post Types
Features
- 18 post types across 6 categories
- Marketplace, Recommendations, Safety & Alerts, Lost & Found, Events & Social, Jobs & Services
- Search, saved posts, and category filtering

Benefits
- Everything local lives in one place
- Structured posts make it easy to act quickly
- Find what you need without noise

### 3) Marketplace & Sharing
Features
- For Sale, Free, Looking to Buy, Borrow/Rent
- Price, condition, pickup/delivery, negotiable pricing

Benefits
- Keeps local trade inside Highwoods
- Reduces waste and encourages sharing
- Faster, safer exchanges with people nearby

### 4) Local Recommendations & Help
Features
- Recommend trades/services, lifestyle tips, or request help
- Ratings, price range, and contact details

Benefits
- Lets good local services rise to the top
- Makes it easier to ask for help
- Builds trust over time

### 5) Safety & Urgent Alerts
Features
- Urgent and security alerts
- Sticky priority banner at the top of the feed

Benefits
- Critical information stays visible
- Helps residents respond quickly to local issues

### 6) Lost & Found
Features
- Lost/found pet and lost/found item posts
- Details like last seen location, reward, and contact info

Benefits
- Faster reunions for pets and belongings
- Neighbours can act immediately with clear info

### 7) Events & Social
Features
- Create events with date/time, venue, max attendees
- RSVP support
- Find a Partner and Discussion posts

Benefits
- More local participation in real-world events
- Turns online connections into real community
- Helps people find activity partners nearby

### 8) Jobs & Services
Features
- Hiring and Looking for Work posts
- Job type, pay rates, skills, experience, and remote options
- Supports paid or unpaid/volunteer roles with negotiable pricing

Benefits
- Keeps local opportunities inside Highwoods
- Easier to find flexible or part-time work nearby

### 9) Direct Messaging & Connections
Features
- One-to-one messaging with read receipts
- Message author from a post
- Connection requests and status tracking
- Report and block users from conversations

Benefits
- Private follow-ups without sharing phone numbers
- Encourages real networking and trust
- Keeps conversations tied to local posts

### 10) Directory
Features
- Local listings with categories (Service, Event, Organisation, Product), ratings, reviews
- Rich profiles with media, contact, and websites

Benefits
- Highlights local businesses and services
- Helps residents make informed choices

### 11) Notifications & Deep Links
Features
- Push notifications for posts, comments, DMs, connection requests, events, alerts
- Deep linking to posts, invites, and directory promos

Benefits
- People don’t miss what matters
- Faster engagement from a single tap

### 12) Personalization & Accessibility
Features
- 7 themes including high-contrast mode
- Saved preferences

Benefits
- Feels personal and accessible to more users

## Key Differentiators
- Invitation-only trust model
- Built specifically for Highwoods (not a generic citywide app)
- Practical post types that solve everyday local problems
- Safety alerts are prioritized, not buried

## Example Use Cases
- “I need a reliable plumber in Highwoods.”
- “Found a black cat near Mill Road.”
- “Selling a child’s bike — local pickup only.”
- “Anyone up for a Saturday morning run?”
- “Urgent: suspicious activity reported on Brinkley Lane.”
